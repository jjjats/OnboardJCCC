Visit the JCCC Campus

Did you know that 75 percent of students who go on an individual tour enroll at Johnson County Community College? The best way to learn about all JCCC has to offer is to experience it in person. Schedule your visit today.

Campus visits are an important step in deciding the right place for you to continue your education.

Cav Leaders conduct individual and group tours. We also offer special event visits designed for high school students, their parents and adult students that can include student panels, financial aid and admission information, academic and career counseling.

Your future awaits you at JCCC!
