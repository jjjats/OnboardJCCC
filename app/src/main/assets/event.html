Campus Visit Group Events

In addition to our daily campus visits, JCCC also has several campus visit events throughout academic year for high school students and their parents.

Register Online for One of the Following Events

Dates and times are listed on the registration form. All students who register for a special campus visit event will receive an email reservation confirmation. Online registration is open for the next two available dates and will be updated after each event is completed.

Home School Events

March 12, 6:30-8:30 p.m.

Learn what it is like to attend JCCC while still in a parent educated environment or after you graduate high school. You will learn what courses are available, when you can begin taking classes and tour the campus. This is specifically targeted to students in high school along with their parents, however, younger siblings are always welcome.

Parent Events

February 24, 6:30-8:30 p.m.

Are you feeling a little overwhelmed when it comes to college planning? Do you see your student possibly attending JCCC, but want to learn more? Do the terms FAFSA, FERPA, College Now, Quick Step start spinning your head? This is the event for you. There will be a variety of sessions for you to choose from, including: JCCC information, your college money plan, financial aid, up and coming careers, and planning for college. We welcome both parents to attend so you may go to as many sessions as interest you.

Adult Students

March 10, 6 p.m.
April 6, 6 p.m.

CC 107, Virginia Krebs Community Room

Finishing your degree is easier than you think; you just need to make the first step. Join this group of adults who are considering going to college for the first time or returning to finish what they started. You are invited to attend a JCCC campus information session to learn how you can take advantage of all the opportunities and services that JCCC offers. We will offer adult campus information sessions for adult students ages 23 and older. The adult sessions will be held during the evenings from 6-7:30 p.m. on the dates listed above.
